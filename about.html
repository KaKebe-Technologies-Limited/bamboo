About Bambo Gallery

Bambo Gallery is a legally registered media company. The Company is registered with URSB with registration Number 207613. Bambo was started by visionary individauls with love and passion for creative arts and Fashion.

The Company partakes provision of Professional Graphics Design, General Printing, Interior Design, Photography & Video Documentaries Services based in Lira City.

Bamboo Gallery seeks to satisfy market demands in Uganda through timely responses and delivery to the general public

The Company deals in Graphics Design and General Printing by creating Visual Communication Materials for client such as Logos, Marketing and Advertising Materials such as FLiers, Brochures, Bussiness Cards, Signages, Wrist Bands, Calenders, among Others. We work with our Clients to identify business goals and objectives, relating strategies and we implement them using Modern State-of-the-art Technology.
